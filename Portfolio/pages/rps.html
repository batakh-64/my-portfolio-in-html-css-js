Rock, Paper, Scissors:

A Classic Game Reimagined for the Web

There’s something timeless about Rock, Paper, Scissors. It's a game we’ve all played — simple rules, instant results, and just the right amount of strategy mixed with luck. Whether it’s settling a friendly debate or just passing the time, this little game has been around for generations.

Rock crushes Scissors
Scissors cuts Paper
Paper covers Rock

Despite how basic it sounds, it’s a great example of game logic, player interaction, and randomness — which makes it perfect for coding practice too.

So, I decided to turn this classic into a web-based game. With a bit of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript magic, I built my own version where you can play against the computer, see who wins, and enjoy a smooth user interface with animations and effects.
